Power to Heat

Using your summer sun to heat in the winter.

How it works

Store summer energy. Use it in winter.

In summer, your solar panels produce more electricity than you can use or store in a battery. With Power to Heat, that surplus electricity is converted into hydrogen using an electrolyser. The hydrogen is stored locally and, when winter comes, converted back into heat through a fuel cell or hydrogen boiler.

This means you can heat your home or business entirely on the solar energy you generated months earlier — no fossil fuels required, no dependency on the grid.

Why Power to Heat?

Seasonal storage

Bridge the gap between summer production and winter demand with long-duration hydrogen storage.

Zero fossil fuels

Eliminate your gas bill entirely. Heat your home or business with 100% locally produced green hydrogen.

Smart EMS control

Our Energy Management System automatically decides when to produce hydrogen and when to release heat, optimising for cost and comfort.
