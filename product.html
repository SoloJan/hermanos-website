One vision. Three products.

It is our mission to use the abundance of green energy to replace fossil fuels wherever they are used. We always produce the hydrogen locally, at your home or business.

The system

Green hydrogen, produced locally.

On windy and sunny periods during the day the green energy supply exceeds the demand, which is leading to very low prices, and sometimes even free electricity. Green energy is cheap energy!

With the energy storage of Hermanos Energy you can benefit from the daily and seasonal price difference and contribute to net zero at the same time. Our system uses an electrolyser to convert surplus electricity into hydrogen, which is stored locally and converted back to energy when you need it.

Choose your application.

Depending on your energy needs, our hydrogen storage system can be configured for heating, electricity, or mobility.
