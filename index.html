Looking to the future.

Renewable energy sources like wind power, and solar are increasing rapidly but to balance the energy grid and secure a stable power supply, the world is still counting on fossil fuel as a energy source. A year-round stable power supply without fossil fuels can only be achieved with local energy storage.

You can help and benefit from the energy transition.

On windy and sunny periods during the day the green energy supply exceeds the demand which is leading to very low prices, and sometimes even free electricity. Green energy is cheap energy! With the energy storage of Hermanos Energy you can benefit from the daily and seasonal price difference and contribute to net zero at the same time.
