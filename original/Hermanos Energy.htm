Looking to the future.

Renewable energy sources like wind power, and solar are increasing rapidly but to balance the energy grid and secure a stable power supply, the world is still counting on fossil fuel as a energy source. A year-round stable power supply without fossil fuels can only be achieved with local energy storage.

You can help and benefit from the energy transition.

On windy and sunny periods during the day the green energy supply exceeds the demand which is leading to very low prices, and sometimes even free electricity. Green energy is cheap energy! With the energy storage of Hermanos Energy you can benefit from the daily and seasonal price difference and contribute to net zero at the same time.

One vision. Three products.

It is our mission to use the abundance of green energy to replace fossil fuels wherever they are used. We always produce the hydrogen locally, at your home or business, and it can be used in various ways.

Using your summer sun to heat in the winter, with power to heat.

All year round green electricyty with power to power.

Tank hydrogen at SAIL Amsterdam

Powered by Hermanos Energy and XNRG.tech

SAIL Amsterdam is one of the biggest maritime events in the world, with more then 10.000 boats, and 2.3 million visitors a day. We are proud to power their fleet of hydrogen boats. XNRG.tech developed a series of hydrogen driven boats for the event which can transport up to 50 people. And while they are out for the day we produce hydrogen, so that they can refuel for the next day when they come back. News channel AT5 made a short video about our installation.
