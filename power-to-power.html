Power to Power

All year round green electricity.

How it works

Green electricity whenever you need it.

With Power to Power, surplus electricity from your solar panels is converted into hydrogen via electrolysis. When the sun isn't shining, a fuel cell converts that stored hydrogen back into electricity — giving you a reliable, grid-independent power supply 24 hours a day, all year round.

Unlike batteries, our hydrogen storage can hold energy for months, not hours. This makes Power to Power ideal for homes, farms, and businesses that want true energy independence.

Why Power to Power?

Long-duration storage

Store weeks or months of energy as hydrogen — far beyond what any battery system can offer.

Grid independence

Reduce or eliminate your reliance on the electricity grid. Generate, store, and use your own clean energy.

Price arbitrage

Our EMS picks the best moments to produce hydrogen based on day-ahead electricity prices, maximising your return.
