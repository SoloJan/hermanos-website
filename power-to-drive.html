Power to Drive

Driving a vehicle on hydrogen.

How it works

Fuel your vehicle with your own solar energy.

With Power to Drive, surplus electricity from your solar panels is used to produce hydrogen on-site via an electrolyser. That hydrogen is stored and dispensed directly into hydrogen fuel cell vehicles — so you can refuel at home or at your business, using energy you generated yourself.

We have demonstrated this at SAIL Amsterdam 2024, where our system fuelled a fleet of hydrogen-powered passenger boats — powering over 50 people per trip, with hydrogen produced the day before each run.

Why Power to Drive?

On-site refuelling

Refuel your hydrogen vehicle at home or at your business — no trips to a filling station required.

Zero emissions

Your vehicle emits only water vapour. Combined with locally produced green hydrogen, your total carbon footprint is zero.

Proven in the field

We powered hydrogen boats at SAIL Amsterdam 2024, demonstrating our system's reliability at scale.
